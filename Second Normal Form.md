**Second Normal Form**



A table is said to be in second normal form if it is in 1st normal form and as well as full functional dependency. No partial dependency.



*Key Terminologies:*

i)Primary key

ii)Composite key

iii)Non prime attributes

iv)Full Functional dependency



*Rules for 2nd normal form:*

1)1nf compliance

ii)No partial dependency



*Transforming to 2nd normal form:*

Just split the table into two



*Benefits of 2nd normal form:*

i)No partial dependency

ii)Reduces redundancy

iii)Data consistency

iv)Easy maintenance and update
