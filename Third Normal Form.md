**Third Normal Form**

A table is said to be in third normal form if it is in 2nd normal form and as well as non transitively dependent.

Non prime attributes should not depend on other non prime attributes.



*key words:*

i)Transitive dependency

ii)Functional dependency



*Transforming into 3rd normal form:*

split the tables into multiple relations(tables) to remove transitive dependency.



*Benefits of 3rd normal form:*

i)Removes transitive dependency

ii)Simplifies inserting and updating

iii)Reduces redundancy

iv)Ensures data integrity
